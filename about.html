Our Foundation

Key Pillars of FAS

Our society stands tall on four fundamental pillars that guide our operations, outreach, and impact within and beyond the university.

Activities & Engagement

Workshops, inter-college events, and competitions.

Branding & Identity

Enhancing visibility online and offline with strong visuals.

Collaboration & Outreach

Partnerships with societies, alumni, and community.

Cultural Focus

Honoring traditions while embracing contemporary trends.

Core Values

Creativity & Innovation

Encouraging original thought, experimentation, and artistic exploration across diverse mediums.

Excellence

Striving for high standards in both artistic pursuits and organizational initiatives.

Inclusivity & Diversity

Welcoming all voices and respecting varied cultural and artistic traditions.

Collaboration & Community

Building meaningful connections among members and with the larger community.

Cultural Preservation

Honoring traditions while embracing contemporary trends, blending the old with the new.

Expression & Impact

Recognizing art as a language of emotion, identity, and social awareness to inspire change.

Department Structure

The Wings of FAS

Creative & Organizing

The core of FAS. Translates ideas into tangible initiatives. conceptualizes workshops, in-house events, and competitions ensuring creativity and efficiency.

Technical & Editorial

Strengthens FAS's digital presence. Manages creative design, content production, and editorial content to uphold a modern and vibrant identity.

MAD Wing

"Make a Difference". Extends initiatives beyond campus through NGO collaborations and socially impactful projects, using art for positive change.

Corporate Communication

The professional interface. Builds partnerships, fosters collaborations, and maintains alumni relations to enhance visibility and credibility.

Our Flagship Events

Main Event

Artisia

The flagship event of the Fine Arts Society. A vibrant platform for students to showcase creativity and talent. It brings together participants fostering healthy competition, collaboration, and celebration of diverse artistic expressions.

Mid-Year Event

Halloween Celebration

Engaging mid-year activities that encourage creativity and fun. Featuring themed artistic activities, costume contests, and interactive sessions, creating a lively and inclusive atmosphere on campus.
